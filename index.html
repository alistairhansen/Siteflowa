🌐 We handle your domain name — hassle free

We'll find the perfect domain name for your business and show you the available options. The cost is covered in your setup fee as long as it's under $50/year. If your chosen domain costs more than that, the difference will be added as a small yearly charge to your account — we'll always let you know the price before anything is confirmed. Either way, we renew it automatically every year for as long as you're on any of our plans. One less thing on your plate.

All plans include your website built and hosted by us. Upgrading your plan unlocks more dashboard features and pages — your website content always stays exactly as built. Downgrading simply hides dashboard features; nothing is removed from your site.
