🌐 We handle your domain name — hassle free

We find the perfect domain for your business and show you available options. The domain cost is included in your setup fee. Here's how yearly renewals work:

Basic
Free subdomain only (yourname.sitefloa.com). No custom domain included.
Standard
Domain free if under $30/yr. If it costs more, you only pay the difference above $30 as a yearly charge.
Premium
Domain free if under $80/yr. If it costs more, you only pay the difference above $80 as a yearly charge.

We renew your domain automatically every year for as long as you're subscribed. One less thing to worry about.

All plans include your website built and hosted by us. Upgrading your plan unlocks more dashboard features and pages — your website content always stays exactly as built. Downgrading simply hides dashboard features; nothing is removed from your site.
